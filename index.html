Inclusive Play

The Playground Project strives to increase the number and quality of inclusive playgrounds in Boston to ensure that accessibility is a key component of playground designs.

We collaborate with the Boston Parks and Recreation Department, research inclusive play equipment, and score playgrounds throughout Boston.

Alex Castillo is a third year student from Huntington, New York and is enrolled in the D’Amore McKim School of Business at Northeastern. An MIS and Entrepreneurship major, Alex hopes to one day start his own online business. He is currently an undergraduate manager for the Northeastern men’s basketball team and is searching for his second co-op. In his free time, Alex enjoys playing basketball, volunteering, running, and skiing.
